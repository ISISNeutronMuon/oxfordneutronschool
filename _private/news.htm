   News

5 September 2005
Final programme available

We have released the final programme for the school lectures which start tomorrow. Professor Peter Day from the Royal Institution of Great Britain will give an evening lecture on Sir Michael Faraday and Dr Christopher Frost will give a lecture on inelastic neutron scattering using single crystals at pulsed neutron sources.

11July 2005
Final place allocations

Emails have been sent to all applicants notifying them of their place allocation. Please remember, that if you have been allocated a place, we need you to confirm that you will attend by 31 July 2005. If we do not hear from you, your place will be allocated to students on the reserve list.

6 July 2005
Registration closed

We have closed registration for the Oxford School due to an unprecedented number of applicants. There are approximately 4 students registered for each available place at the School. Selection will take place later this week.

4 July 2005
Updated selection process timetable

In order to allow students to know if they have a place at the School and take advantage of cheap travel deals, we have updated our selection timetable. We will send out emails and letters to students around 11 July.

28 June 2005
Registration closing date brought forward

Demand for places on the School has been unprecedented this year and we are already 4 times oversubscribed. We are trying to increase the number of places available but we are not going to be able to offer places to everyone.

As a result of the oversubscription, we will be closing registration at 00:00 BST 6 July 2005, instead of the previously advertised date of 1 August.

Please make sue that in the comments box, you include some information on how you think attending the course will benefit your studies.

13 June 2005
Second Announcement

We have issued the second announcement. Apologies for the slight delay whilst we were awaiting some final finance information. We are pleased to be able to offer places on the school at a total cost per student of £ 325 (€ 486) thanks to the generous support of CCLRC and NMI3. Full registration is now open.

13 June 2005
Lecturers & Programme

We are pleased to announce the full list of lecturers at this years school, together with the final programme.
