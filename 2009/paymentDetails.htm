Payment Details

The total cost per residential student is £ 480  (€ 556) which includes accommodation, breakfast and evening meals, course materials, evening drinks reception and gala dinner.

The total cost per non-residential student is £ 225 (€ 260) which includes course materials, evening drinks reception and gala dinner.

We can accept payment by electronic bank transfer or credit card. Follow the instructions below for your chosen payment method.

Payment must be made by 12 September 2009.
